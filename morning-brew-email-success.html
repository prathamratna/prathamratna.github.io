How Morning Brew Achieved 40%+ Email Open Rates in 2025

12 min read March 17, 2025 Email Marketing
Email Marketing Newsletter Content Strategy Case Study

In an era where email open rates are declining across industries, Morning Brew has defied the odds by consistently achieving over 40% open rates with their daily newsletter. With a subscriber base of 4 million readers and a valuation of $75 million, their email marketing strategy has become a gold standard in the industry. This case study explores how Morning Brew revolutionized email marketing through their unique approach to content, design, and audience engagement.

The Morning Brew Formula: A New Approach to Email Marketing

Morning Brew's success stems from their innovative approach to email marketing, which combines conversational writing, clean design, and strategic content curation. Their daily newsletter has become a must-read for business professionals, achieving engagement rates that most marketers can only dream of.

Key Elements of Morning Brew's Email Strategy

Morning Brew's email success is built on several strategic pillars:

1. Conversational Tone and Voice

Their content strategy is characterized by:

  • Casual, friendly writing style
  • Use of humor and wit
  • Personalized content delivery
  • Consistent brand voice across all emails

2. Content Strategy

Morning Brew's content approach focuses on:

  • Curated business news with unique insights
  • Bite-sized, digestible information
  • Clear value proposition in every email
  • Perfect balance of information and entertainment
"We don't just report the news; we make it enjoyable to read. Our goal is to be the friend who explains complex business concepts in a way that's both informative and entertaining." - Morning Brew Team

3. Design and User Experience

Their email design strategy includes:

  • Clean, minimalist layout
  • Mobile-first approach
  • Strategic use of white space
  • Consistent branding elements

Case Study: The Referral Program Success

One of Morning Brew's most successful strategies was their referral program, which:

  • Offered exclusive rewards for sharing
  • Created a sense of community
  • Generated 60% of new subscribers
  • Increased engagement through social proof

Key Takeaways

  • Content is King: Focus on creating valuable, engaging content that resonates with your audience
  • Design Matters: Invest in clean, user-friendly email design
  • Consistency is Key: Maintain a consistent brand voice and delivery schedule
  • Community Building: Create opportunities for audience engagement and sharing
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Use analytics to understand and optimize performance

Conclusion

Morning Brew's success demonstrates that email marketing is far from dead. Their innovative approach to content, design, and audience engagement has set a new standard for email marketing in the digital age. By focusing on delivering value through a unique voice and consistent experience, they've built one of the most successful email newsletters in the industry.
